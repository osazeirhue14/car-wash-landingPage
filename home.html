Valeting

Valeting is a comprehensive car cleaning service that goes beyond a standard car wash. It involves a deep and thorough cleaning of both
the interior and exterior of a vehicle, often
including polishing, waxing, and detailing to restore the car to a near showroom-like condition.

Car Wash

A car wash is a process of cleaning the exterior and sometimes the interior of a vehicle
to remove dirt, dust, grime, and other contaminants. It can be done manually or automatically using specialized equipment.
